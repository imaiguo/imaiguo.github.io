我们今日在主恢复中的当前急务

中文标语

我们今日在主恢复中的当前急务

一九九六年六月台湾区众地方召会相调大会主仆李常受弟兄的交通

一、我们要实际的进入新约中神圣启示的第三大段落。

二、我们要靠着主七倍加强的灵，起来答应主在启示录二、三章的呼召，作主在今天的得胜者。

三、我们要借着主七倍加强之灵的全备供应，愿出代价，不顾反对，而全面的把基督宇宙的身体建造起来，以完成神永远的心意，而达到k最终的目的，就是新耶路撒冷，作k神圣的扩增并彰显，直到永远。

英文标语

Our Present Urgent Duties in the Lord's Recovery Today

Brother Lee's Fellowship for the Blending Conference of the Churches in Taiwan, June 1996

1. We need to enter practically into the third great section of the divine revelation in the New Testament.

2. By the Lord's sevenfold intensified Spirit we need to rise up and answer the Lord's call in Revelation 2 and 3 to be the overcomers today.

3. Through the bountiful supply of the Lord's sevenfold intensified Spirit, we need to be willing to pay the price and not to care for any opposition that we may build up the universal Body of Christ in all aspects to accomplish the eternal intention of God and attain His ultimate purpose, which is the New Jerusalem as His divine enlargement and expression for eternity.
