过照着圣经中神圣启示高峰之生活实行的路

中文标语

过照着圣经中神圣启示高峰之生活实行的路

一九九四年十一月感恩节特会

借着基督复活的大模成祂的死－腓三10。

借着耶稣基督之灵全备的供应，活基督以显大基督－腓一19下～21上。

模成神儿子的形像，使祂儿子在许多弟兄中作长子－罗八29。

借着在一切事上长到元首基督里面，建造基督的身体－弗四15～16。

英文标语

The Practical Way to Live a Life According to the High Peak of the Divine Revelation in the Holy Scriptures

Thanksgiving Day Conference, November 1994

To be conformed to the death of Christ by the power of His resurrection －Phil.3:10.

To live Christ for His magnification by the bountiful supply of the Spirit of Jesus Christ－Phil.1:19b-21a.

To be conformed to the image of God's Son, that He might be the Firstborn among many brothers－Rom.8:29.

To build up the Body of Christ by growing into him, the Head, in all things－Eph.4:15-16.
