如何作同工与长老并如何履行同工与长老的义务

中文标语

如何作同工与长老并如何履行同工与长老的义务

一九九六年十月同工长老特会

基督丰满的职事，乃是为着成就神永远的经纶，完成于三个时期。

在第一个成肉体的时期中，将神带到人里面，在人性中彰显神，并完成k法理的救赎。

在第二个总括的时期中，生为神的长子，成为赐生命的灵，并为着k的身体产生众信徒。

在第三个加强的时期中，加强k生机的救恩，产生得胜者，并完成新耶路撒冷。

英文标语

How to Be a Co-Worker and an Elder and How to Fulfill Their Obligations

Co-workers' and Elders' Conference, October 1996

The full ministry of Christ is carried out in three stages for the fulfillment of God's eternal economy.

In the first stage of incarnation to bring God into man, to express God in humanity, and to accomplish His judicial redemption.

In the second stage of inclusion to be begotten as God's firstborn Son, to become the life-giving Spirit, and to regenerate the believers for His Body.

In the third stage of intensification to intensify His organic salvation, to produce the overcomers, and to consummate the New Jerusalem.
